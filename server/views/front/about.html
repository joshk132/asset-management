How many people are there in team?

There are 3 of us currently but we are always looking to expand our team as we grow. Having a larger team means that we can add more features and grow our user base to get more feedback on how to improve.

What is our history?

Our founder Josh searched for a cloud based asset management software that was affordable and simple to use. Learning new software takes a long time and he didn't want to commit to learning a large site that was overpriced.

How did it start?

This was a personal project that when shown to friends and co-workers, Josh got good feedback and praise. Upon this he decided that he needed to offer his creation to others and help small businesses know more about their business and it's assets.

What are our achievements?

We have excellent customer service and focus to make the customer experience something that they won't soon forget. We take every bit of feedback and look at how we can use it to improve upon what we have.
